About Us

Welcome to Big Dave's Chop Shop, where you can enjoy some good grub while we repair your bike. Big Dave's Chop Shop was created by chef and motorcyle rider, David "Big Dave" Lenbo. While riding his Indian Dark Horse, Big Dave caught a flat tire. While waiting for his noble steed to be fixed, Big Dave grew an appettite for some good ol' BBQ. Unfortunately, the nearest diner was 15 miles down the road and the idea came to Big Dave
